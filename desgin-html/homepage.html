Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How does the AI favicon generator work?

expand_more

Our AI uses advanced machine learning models to interpret your text description and generate a unique favicon design. You simply type in what you're looking for—like "a minimalist mountain logo"—and the AI creates several options for you to choose from.

What file formats can I download?

expand_more

Depending on your plan, you can download favicons in various formats, including PNG (in multiple sizes like 16x16, 32x32, etc.) and high-resolution SVG. Our Pro and Business plans offer SVG downloads for crisp, scalable icons.

Can I use the favicons for commercial projects?

expand_more

Yes! Favicons generated under our Pro and Business plans come with a full commercial license, allowing you to use them for any business or client project without attribution.

What if I'm not happy with the generated favicons?

expand_more

You can generate multiple sets of favicons by tweaking your text prompt. Our free plan allows for 10 generations per month, while paid plans offer unlimited generations, so you can keep trying until you find the perfect design.

Do I need any design skills to use this tool?

expand_more

Not at all! Our tool is designed for everyone, regardless of their design experience. If you can describe what you want, our AI can create it for you. It's the simplest way to get a professional favicon.
